The purpose of this website

Our website was made to share useful descriptions and tips about the various lessons, parts and inner workings of the english language. This webpage was made in collaboration with the various people in my group (Group White) and it wouldnt really be possible without their help.

How to get started?

To get started, simply click the "topics" link above that will bring you to the list of topics that we have created, and from there you can click on the topic that you want to study. An optional test is present if you want to test your skill with the topics, Have fun!
